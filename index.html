About Vaidyuthak


The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering of The National Institute of Engineering, Mysuru, is delighted to announce the highly anticipated return of the ECE Fest after a four-year hiatus. The last Fest was held in the year 2019, and we are thrilled to bring it back in a grand manner in 2023, under the exciting name Vaidyuthak 2023. After overcoming challenges posed by Covid-19 in previous years, we are determined to make this year's fest a memorable and impactful experience. As a part of the ECE Fest, we are organizing a state-level Technical event, featuring a Project Exhibition and Poster Presentation.

In recognition of the outstanding contributions, we are pleased to announce the following prize money:


Project Exhibition :
1st Place: ₹7000/-
2nd Place: ₹5000/-

Poster Presentation :
1st Place: ₹5000/-
2nd Place: ₹3000/-

Join us for an exciting showcase of innovation and expertise in the field. Don't miss the opportunity to be part of this significant event in 2023, as we bring back the spirit of Vaidyuthak with enthusiasm and grandeur!
